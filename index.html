Mike Trout

The Best Baseball Player of Our Generation?

Mike Trout could potentially be one of the greatest baseball players of all time. There is no close competition for his five-tool ability combined with his consistency in today's game. His statistics are very similar to some of the best hitters in the history of baseball when they were his age.

Below is a table comparing Trout's stats through his age 25 season with the stats of: Barry Bonds, Mickey Mantle, Hank Aaron, and Babe Ruth through their respective age 25 seasons.
